CS 4010 Grade Calculator

What does this do?
  • Calculates your total extra credit, total homework, quiz, test, and project grades, and overall grade.
  • Does not save your scores in any way.
  • Calculations are only done on this browser.

How do I use this?

You only need to input the exact scores you have in Canvas (e.g., 90, 100, etc.). Decimals and percents will not work. I've also changed the max project score from 10 to 15 (e.g., use 15 if you got a perfect score on a project).
